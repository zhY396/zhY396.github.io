句子

  • An analysis by the Tax Policy Center published this week estimated that middle-income families, those making 51,000 $to$91,000 per year, will see their after-tax income rise by 5.5 percent as a result of the tax changes and stimulus payments in the legislation.

    一项由TPC在这个星期公布的分析估计，中等收入家庭一年收入51000~91000美元，在税收优惠和立法方面的刺激消费以后，他们的税后收入将会提高5.5%。

    考虑到美国不同地区的生活成本差异很大，将救助计划精准定位并帮助那些稳居中产阶级的人是一项挑战。

    结构: … has been challenging, given … varies widely in different parts of the ….

    鉴于…差异很大，…变得有挑战性

    例句: Finding a decent and comfortable job has been challenging, given the salary varies widely in different parts of the China.
